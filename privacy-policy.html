Privacy Policy

Web CPE is committed to protecting your information and privacy. We recognize our obligation to keep your information secure and inform you how we use it. We use your information solely to communicate with you on our CPE programs and to provide certificates of completion. We do not sell or share your any personal information with any third parties. We use secured servers to protect your information. This Privacy policy may be updated from time to time to address any new concerns.

Information We Collect

When you register in our system to use it to meet your CPE needs you provide us with some personally identifiable information that you knowingly choose to disclose. We ask you to submit your name, address, email address and other information, which we store in a secured server. We also request your name and email address if you contact us via the "Contact Us" link, and we may keep a record of users who have contacted us so that we can respond properly to any future questions.

Use of Your Information We Collect

We will use your information to provide you with the information or content that you have requested. In some cases, we may contact you about our products or services. We do not sell, trade or rent any personal information to third parties at any time. If you do not want to receive any information from us, you may alert us through our "Contact Us" link to change your preferences. We may use non-identifying information collected on our website in the aggregate to better understand the use of the website and to improve on the website appearance, design and content. We may hire individuals and/or other companies to perform work on our behalf. All individuals who have access to personally identifiable information are required to protect the information in a manner that is consistent with this Privacy Statement.

We may provide access to your personally identifiable information when legally required to do so, to cooperate with police investigations or other legal proceedings, to protect against misuse or unauthorized use of our website, to limit our legal liability and protect our rights, or to protect the rights and safety of visitors to this website or the public. In those instances, the information is provided only for that limited purpose

Information Customers Can Access

You can modify, update or delete (non-required items) your profile information at any time by visiting the “Edit Profile” link in customer dashboard. It is very important that your email address is current and correct. We communicate with you (like sending certificates and program information) primarily through email. You can see information on your both completed and incomplete courses.

Protection of Your Information

We authorize only authorized limited number of persons to access our database servers. Our servers are backed up regularly and protected by security systems. For security we use PayPal to execute financial transactions. After a transaction, your private information (credit cards, social security numbers, financials, etc.) is not be stored on our servers. That information is secured in the PayPal system. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology that encrypts the data you provide to ensure privacy. However, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Accordingly, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information that you transmit to us, so you do so at your own risk.

Cookies

To provide better service and a more effective website, our website utilizes standard technology called "cookies". A cookie is a small text file placed on your computer's hard drive by our web page server. Cookies are a feature of web browser software that allows web servers to recognize the computer used to access a website. Cookies are commonly used on websites and do not harm your system. We may also use web server logs to collect information about how our website is used. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information, and we do not combine information collected through cookies with any other information to determine who you are. You can prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, notify you when you receive a new cookie, or disable cookies altogether. However, if you disable cookies some parts of our website may not operate correctly.

Links to Other Websites

Only for your convenience, we may provide links to other websites, like NASBA, IRS, and State Boards of Accountancy.

We Are Register With

Web CPE is registered with the National Association of State Boards of Accountancy (NASBA) as a sponsor of continuing professional education on the National Registry of CPE Sponsors. State boards of accountancy have final authority on the acceptance of individual courses for CPE credit.
