About us

Web CPE has been offering CPE since 2000. Web CPE is registered with the NASBA as a self-study CPE course provider. Web CPE is also an IRS approved CE course provider to Enrolled Agents.

Web CPE is managed by a retired CPA, who understands the importance of quality CPE to maintain the sharp edges of CPAs, EAs, and other tax return preparers. The primary emphasis of the company is to provide quality CPE courses conveniently at reasonable prices. Web CPE maintains its focus on customer satisfaction and has a 90-day full refund policy with no questions asked.
